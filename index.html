Comprehensive Healthcare Solutions

Nine integrated features designed to transform rural healthcare delivery and accessibility.

Video/Audio Consultation

Secure, low-bandwidth video and audio consultations that connect rural patients with doctors instantly — eliminating long travel and wait times.

Multilingual Availability

Smart multilingual interface with real-time translation support, so patients can speak in Punjabi/Hindi and doctors can reply in English — breaking down language barriers.

Pharmacy Stock Tracking System

A live medicine inventory dashboard for local pharmacies. Tracks availability, flags low stock, and automates restocking alerts to ensure no patient goes without medicines.

AI Symptom Checker / Chatbot

An AI-powered chatbot for first-level triage. Patients describe their symptoms, and the system suggests possible causes or directs them to the right specialist — reducing clinic overload.

Prescription to Reminders – Never Miss a Dose

Converts prescriptions into daily reminders. Patients get SMS/app alerts in their local language for every dose, improving treatment adherence and recovery outcomes.

Digital Health Records

A centralized, secure platform that stores patient history, test results, and prescriptions — accessible to both doctors and patients anytime, anywhere.

Doctor Pool Scheduler

An intelligent scheduling system that balances doctor availability across teleconsultations and clinics — minimizing idle time and maximizing patient coverage.

Emergency Alert App with Location Sharing

A one-tap SOS system that shares live patient location with doctors, ambulances, and family members — ensuring quick response in medical emergencies.

Digital Health Card (QR-based)

QR-based health identity cards for every patient. Scanning reveals essential details like blood group, allergies, and last checkup — life-saving during emergencies.
